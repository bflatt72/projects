Personal Details

Sheldon is an eccentric character who has been thought to reside on the autism spectrum. As such, he can be very difficult to live with. He also can exhibit narcissistic and ego-centric behavior. He routinely lets everyone know that he is the smartest one in the room and he has a plethora of annoying quirks and idiosyncracies.

However, he has a very good heart and is loyal and trustworthy. He is currently engaged to his girlfriend of many years, Amy Farrah Fowler.

He lives with his best friend and fellow physicist Leonard Hoffstetter and his wife Penny. His other friends include engineer Howard Wolowitz and his wife Bernadette, Rajesh Koothrapali, Wil Wheaton, comic book store owner Stuart, and none other than Stephen Hawking.
